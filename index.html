Our process

From conversation to deployed agents,
in five focused steps.

Every Nakera engagement follows the same structure. No improvisation, no scope creep, no surprises. Clarity at every stage so you know where you stand and what comes next.

STEP 01

A 30-minute conversation, not a sales pitch.

We start by understanding your operations: where time goes, where mistakes happen, and which workflows are eating your team alive. No deck, no pressure, just questions.

  • Map your current operational pain points
  • Identify which tasks are AI candidates
  • Honest read on whether Nakera is the right fit
Duration: 30 minutes
STEP 02

We map your operations. You walk away with a blueprint.

We cartograph your workflows, score every use case by impact and feasibility, and deliver a detailed strategic blueprint. You keep this document either way, whether we build together or not.

  • Process cartography across your stack
  • Use case scoring: impact times feasibility
  • Implementation roadmap with timelines
Duration: 1 to 2 weeks
STEP 03

Custom agents built into your existing stack.

Weekly sprints with regular demos. You see the work as it happens, not just at the end. Every agent is designed around your actual workflows, not generic SaaS templates.

  • Weekly sprint cycles with live demos
  • Native integration with your existing tools
  • Built-in safeguards and human-in-the-loop checkpoints
Duration: 2 to 6 weeks per agent
STEP 04

Production deployment, full team enablement.

We deploy to production, integrate with your live systems, and train your team on how to work alongside the agents. We stay on-site or in remote sync until everything runs smoothly.

  • Production deployment with safe rollout
  • Team training and documentation
  • Monitoring dashboards from day one
Duration: 1 week
STEP 05

Your agents get smarter with every interaction.

We monitor performance, gather your team's feedback, and continuously improve the agents. This isn't a one-shot deployment. It's a partnership that compounds over time.

  • Performance monitoring and analytics
  • Iterative improvements based on real usage
  • Scaling support as your needs grow
Duration: Ongoing
